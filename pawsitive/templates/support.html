Community and School Partnerships

School Engagement Program

We offer free educational presentations and resources for schools across Victoria. Our trained educators can visit your school to deliver age-appropriate lessons on biodiversity, conservation, and responsible pet ownership.

Students will learn about local wildlife, threats to biodiversity, and how they can take action to protect native species in their own communities.

Request a School Visit

Community Group Collaboration

We work with community groups, gardening clubs, landcare organizations, and other local initiatives to amplify conservation efforts. Together, we can develop and implement local projects that benefit native wildlife.

We can provide training, resources, and guidance to help your group create wildlife-friendly spaces and raise awareness in your community.
