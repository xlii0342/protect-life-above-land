üß¨ Impact Beyond Wildlife

Leadbeater's Possum, a critically endangered species in Victoria, faces extinction due to habitat destruction from logging and bushfires. This unique marsupial relies on mature mountain ash forests, which are rapidly disappearing.

Feral cats pose a significant threat to Leadbeater's Possum by hunting them and spreading diseases like toxoplasmosis. Urgent action is needed to manage feral cat populations and protect Victoria's biodiversity, ensuring the survival of this precious species.
