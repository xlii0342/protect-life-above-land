Effective Report Example

Location: Near the southern entrance of Dandenong Ranges National Park, approximately 500 meters from the parking lot on a small trail

Date and Time: March 15, 2025, around 4:30 PM

Description: Observed a medium-sized tabby feral cat stalking through the undergrowth. The cat was approximately 40-50 cm long with a robust build. When I saw it, it was pursuing a small native bird. I managed to take a photo, which although slightly blurry, clearly shows its striped pattern. This is the third time I've seen similar cats in this area.
